ABOUT

Dentistry encompasses the study, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of conditions affecting the oral cavity, including teeth, gums, and supporting structures. Dentists are trained healthcare professionals who offer a range of oral health services, from routine check-ups and cleanings to restorative procedures like fillings and crowns, surgical interventions such as extractions and dental implants, as well as cosmetic treatments like teeth whitening and veneers. The ultimate aim of dentistry is to maintain good oral health, prevent dental issues, and enhance both function and aesthetics of the mouth.
